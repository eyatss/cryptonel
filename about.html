Who We Are?

Cryptonel is a marketing-focused affiliate network built by professionals with hands-on experience in financial lead generation and digital performance marketing. We do not provide investment advice or manage funds. Our role is simple and clear: we connect potential investors with licensed and well-established trading platforms based on their interests and preferences. By combining data-driven marketing strategies with global outreach, we help platforms grow — and users discover suitable opportunities.


Cryptonel is also a global affiliate and partnership company focused on providing high-quality client acquisition solutions for online investment and trading call centers.


We operate exclusively in the B2B sector and collaborate with brokers, platforms, and call center operations seeking scalable and performance-driven growth.
